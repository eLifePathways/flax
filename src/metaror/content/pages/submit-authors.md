Thank you for considering MetaResearch Open Review (MetaROR) for the peer review of your scholarly work. MetaROR is dedicated to enhancing the quality and transparency of academic research through our Open Peer Review service, following the Publish, Review, Curate model. Our platform facilitates a constructive dialogue between authors and reviewers, ensuring that your work receives the recognition and critical feedback it deserves. Below are the detailed instructions for submitting your preprint to MetaROR.

## Preparation of Your Manuscript

Before submitting to MetaROR, your manuscript should first be published on a preprint server of your choice. This initial step ensures that your research is promptly shared with the scholarly community, aligning with our commitment to open access and the rapid dissemination of research findings.

- Select a Preprint Server: Choose a preprint server relevant to your field of study. Ensure that the server you select complies with your funding body's requirements, if applicable.
- Preprint Publication: Follow the chosen preprint server's guidelines for submitting your manuscript. Once published, take note of the DOI (Digital Object Identifier) or URL of your preprint, as this will be necessary for the submission process to MetaROR.


## Submission Process to MetaROR

- **Account Registration:** If you are a first-time user of MetaROR, you will have the opportunity to create an account when you choose to submit your research.
- **Initiating the Review Process:** After logging in, navigate to the "Submit Your Preprint" section. You will be required to fill in the submission form, which includes providing the DOI or URL of your preprint, a brief summary of your research, and selecting relevant keywords.
- **Confirmation:** Upon submission, you will receive a confirmation email with details regarding the next steps and what to expect from the review process.

## Review Process

- **Open Review:** Your submission will be subjected to our open peer review process, where appointed reviewers will provide constructive feedback aimed at enhancing the quality and impact of your work.
- **Interaction:** Authors are encouraged to engage with reviewers' comments to foster an academic dialogue, further refining their manuscript based on received feedback.

## No Submission Costs

Please note that there are no costs associated with submitting your preprint to MetaROR for review. Our platform is committed to removing financial barriers to the publication process, ensuring that all researchers have the opportunity to participate in and benefit from open scholarly communication.

We look forward to your submission and to supporting the dissemination and enhancement of your research through the MetaROR platform. If you have any questions or require further assistance, please do not hesitate to contact us at enquiries@metaror.org.

Warm regards,

The MetaROR Team
